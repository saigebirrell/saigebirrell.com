what to make in the kitchen
make your own cloth shopping bags and homemade cloth produce bags for produce and bulk foods like pasta or cat food
make your own dish liquid or use solely baking soda, which can be used for baking and washing pots and pans
make dish rags from old cotton t-shirts
make a veggie garden or start small and make a herb garden
make a broth from old vegetable scraps before putting them in the compost
make your own nut or oat milk by soaking almonds or oats, then draining them through a sieve and into a bottle
