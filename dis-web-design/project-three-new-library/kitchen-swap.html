what to swap in the kitchen
swap glad wrap for beeswax wrap
swap single-use containers for glass jars and fill them with bulk items like flour, seeds, nuts, spices, tea, honey and coconut oil
swap tea bags and coffee sachets for a french press, which you can also use to strain homemade milk
swap paper towel for a recycled option; the company Who Gives a Crap provides 100% recycled paper towel
swap bottled water for a refillable jug in the fridge
swap plastic bags and containers for reusable tubs, especially when ordering takeout or visiting the butchers
swap plastic milk bottles by investing in a reusable glass jug and making your own at home
swap dish scrubs for cloths or a wooden bottle brush
swap a plastic lunchbox for a stainless steel lunchbox
swap bamboo skewers for metal skewers
visit a bulk food store and take back your egg carton to refill
