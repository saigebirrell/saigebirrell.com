what to swap in the household
swap plastic toys for recycled books, wooden toys and purchase from sustainable toy companies
attend clothing swap events or swap clothes items with friends
swap large department stores for local brands (local eco-friendly clothing stores focus on avoiding materials like polyester, nylon and acrylic which is bad for the environment)
swap purchasing magazines for visiting the library or cafe, or save money and read online
swap cotton for linen in terms of bed sheets and sets (linen uses less water)
swap purchasing newpapers for a digital subscription
swap laundry detergent for a visit to the bulk store and refill a glass jar (this saves all the scooping cups collected over the years)
swap common sunscreens for organic mineral sunscreens
swap pet litter for litter made of sustainable materials like recycled newspaper or even coconut
swap common light bulbs for energy-efficient, long-lasting light bulbs (remember natural light too)
