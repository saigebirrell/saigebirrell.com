what to avoid in the household
avoid shopping for new items and buy second-hand where you can
avoid online shopping (heavy single use packaging)
avoid cleaning products in single-use bottles
avoid magazines
avoid wrapping paper
avoid medical and cleaning gloves
avoid single-use laundry detergent
avoid cotton bed sets and sheets
avoid common sunscreens (contribute to coral bleaching)
avoid single-use air fresheners
avoid leaving applicances plugged in
